Accessibility Commitment

ISE Platform is designed to be accessible to all children, including those with autism spectrum disorder and intellectual disabilities. Our accessibility features are based on WCAG guidelines and input from special needs families.

  • Screen reader compatible
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Clear visual indicators
  • Consistent navigation patterns
